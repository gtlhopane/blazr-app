POPIA Compliance Policy

Blazr Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Blazr is a registered private cannabis social club and delivery service platform. As part of our commitment to legal compliance and member trust, we are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Who This Policy Applies To

This policy applies to all members of the Blazr private club, including:

Individual members (customers)

Fulfillment members (drivers)

Merchant members (club stores)

2. What Personal Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to verify membership and facilitate secure delivery:

Full name

Identification number or document

Contact details (email, phone)

Delivery address

Proof of membership (in cases of merchant/driver onboarding)

Order history and platform activity

3. Why We Collect This Information

We use your information to:

Verify legal membership eligibility

Process and deliver member orders

Provide driver assignments and route logistics

Manage member accounts

Send service-related updates

Improve functionality and service experience

We do not sell or share your information with third-party advertisers or external databases.

4. How Your Information Is Stored and Protected

All data is securely stored on encrypted, access-controlled servers. Only authorized Blazr personnel may access your data for purposes related to membership verification, delivery logistics, or compliance obligations.

5. Sharing Information Within the Club Framework

We may share your details only within the private Blazr club network:

With verified drivers, to complete your delivery

With internal compliance auditors

With club stores from which you place an order

This sharing remains within the closed-loop Blazr network.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject, you have the right to:

Request access to your personal data

Correct or update your information

Request deletion of your personal information

Object to certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: privacy@blazr.co

7. Cookies and Tracking

Blazr uses limited cookies to:

Authenticate sessions

Improve user experience

Track analytics (anonymously)

You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

8. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be communicated through your registered contact details or platform notices.

9. Contact Us

For questions, concerns, or access requests, please contact: Blazr Privacy Office Email: privacy@blazr.co

Blazr is committed to respecting your privacy while operating legally as a private cannabis club under South African law.
